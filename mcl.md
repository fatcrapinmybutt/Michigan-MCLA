# Michigan Compiled Laws (MCL) — Court-Practice Reference

**Abbreviation:** MCL  
**Description:** The official codification of Michigan statutory law. The MCL contains thousands of individual statutes organized by chapter. This reference covers the chapters and sections most frequently encountered in court practice, litigation, and legal proceedings in Michigan.  
**Citation format:** MCL [chapter number].[section number] (e.g., MCL 600.2116)

## MCL Chapter 600 — Revised Judicature Act of 1961 (RJA)

The **Revised Judicature Act** (RJA) is the primary statute governing the structure, jurisdiction, and procedure of Michigan courts.  
**Citation:** MCL 600.101 et seq.

### Court Structure and Jurisdiction

| Section | Title | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| **MCL 600.101** | Short Title | Establishes the short title 'Revised Judicature Act of 1961.' |
| **MCL 600.151** | One Court of Justice | Establishes the unified Michigan 'One Court of Justice' system. |
| **MCL 600.201** | Supreme Court; Jurisdiction | Supreme Court has general superintending control over all inferior courts. |
| **MCL 600.203** | Supreme Court; Composition; Term | 7 justices elected on a nonpartisan ballot to 8-year terms. |
| **MCL 600.209** | Supreme Court; Quorum; Decision | 4 justices constitute a quorum; majority of those sitting required. |
| **MCL 600.223** | Supreme Court; Promulgation of Rules | Supreme Court has power to promulgate rules of procedure and evidence. |
| **MCL 600.301** | Court of Appeals; Establishment | Establishes the Michigan Court of Appeals as an intermediate appellate court. |
| **MCL 600.308** | Court of Appeals; Jurisdiction | Appeals of right from final judgments of circuit, probate, and other courts. |
| **MCL 600.401** | Circuit Court; Establishment | Circuit courts in each county; court of general jurisdiction. |
| **MCL 600.605** | Circuit Court; General Jurisdiction | Original jurisdiction over civil claims exceeding $25,000 and all equity matters. |
